Onion Plant Growth Report

Plant Health

Poor
⚠️ Affected by Onion Downy Mildew

Soil Nutrition

Nitrogen (N): 12%

Phosphorus (P): 8%

Potassium (K): 10%

Your soil has low NPK levels, which is affecting plant growth.

Treatment Recommendations

  • Apply Mancozeb or Chlorothalonil fungicides to control Onion Downy Mildew.
  • Ensure proper drainage to avoid waterlogging, which exacerbates the disease.
  • Remove and destroy infected plant parts to prevent spread.

Fertilizer Recommendations

Based on your NPK levels, use the following fertilizers available in India:

  • Urea (for Nitrogen deficiency)
  • DAP (Di-Ammonium Phosphate) (for Phosphorus deficiency)
  • Muriate of Potash (MOP) (for Potassium deficiency)

Natural Methods

For organic farming, consider the following natural methods:

  • Use vermicompost or cow dung manure to improve soil fertility.
  • Spray neem oil to control fungal diseases naturally.
  • Apply wood ash to increase Potassium levels in the soil.

Expected Results

By following the above recommendations, you can expect:

  • Improved soil nutrition and plant health within 2-3 weeks.
  • Reduction in disease symptoms and prevention of further spread.
  • Increased yield and quality of onion bulbs.
